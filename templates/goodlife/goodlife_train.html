About Us

We are a group of retired experienced athletes who because of past physical health problem were forced to make a change towards living a healthier lifestyle. Some of us have competed at world sports competitions while some of us stay consistantly living a healthy lifestyle . Now our mission to help everyday people who wants to make a change to have the tools and knowledge they need to acheive a good healtyh lifestyle.

Together we have created Good Life gym to do just that. Sharing all the experiences of diets, training and life style changes we experienced. Our specially trained personnels at Good Life are here to assit you in every way possible and guide you in the right direction to acheive great health and the sustainability you are looking for.

Established in 2022, our facility is new with easy to use equipments. To show our dedication to you, as a new member our staff are also trained to provide you with "How To Use" tour so you never feel like you do not know what you are doing or use the equipments in the wrong way.
